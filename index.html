About us

The Viltrum Empire stands as one of the most feared civilizations in the universe — a race forged through brutal survival. On their home planet, the weak were eradicated, leaving only the strongest to build an empire of unmatched power. The Viltrumites believe strength is the ultimate truth, and mercy is a disease. Their purpose is domination — to bring every world under their rule, believing it to be for the greater good. Led by warriors like Thrag, they conquer galaxies with unmatched might, spreading their ideology of perfection. To defy a Viltrumite is to challenge evolution itself.

CONQUEST

Conquest is a relentless Viltrumite warrior, feared across galaxies for his immense strength and cruelty. Loyal to the Empire, he embodies its ideals of domination and perfection, challenging Invincible at every turn and testing his courage and limits.

THRAGG

Thragg, the Viltrum Empire’s strongest warrior, is ruthless, cunning, and nearly invincible. He embodies Viltrumite ideals of strength, conquest, and domination, relentlessly challenging Invincible and his allies.

BATTLE BEAST

Battle Beast is a fierce, unstoppable alien warrior obsessed with combat. Loyal to strength above all, he seeks worthy opponents, testing Invincible and others in brutal, relentless battles
